Every PL8

Every PL8 is a mobile web game, with an App Store release coming soon, built around the challenge of spotting license plates across America.

Players can collect standard plates from all 50 states, then go deeper with specialty and military plates across select states—from colleges and causes to military branches and more. Think trading card collecting, but for the road.

Spot Every PL8—always drive safe and obey your state's hands-free laws.
