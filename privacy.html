Every PL8 Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Overview

Every PL8 is a license plate spotting game. Your progress is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your data, and we do not share, sell, or transmit your information to anyone.

Location

When you mark a plate as found, the app may request your device’s location to record approximately where you spotted it. Location access is optional — the app works fully without it.

Location data is stored only on your device. We use it to show your spotting history on a map and to award region-based achievements. You can disable location capture at any time from the in-app Settings without affecting your iOS Location Services permission, or revoke the permission entirely from your device’s Settings → Privacy → Location Services.

Game Progress & Local Storage

Your found plates, achievements, preferences, and settings are saved in your device’s local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to use the Export Progress feature, which creates a backup file that you control entirely.

Permissions and Native Features

Every PL8 may request the following device permissions, all of which are optional and used only for the in-app feature noted:

  • Location: to tag where each plate was spotted.
  • Notifications: if enabled in a future release, used only for in-app reminders. Notifications are local to your device — we do not send remote push notifications.

The app also uses standard device features that do not require explicit permission, such as the system Share sheet (to share an installation link or a badge), the system clipboard (to copy text you initiate), the in-app browser (to open external links you tap), the system review prompt (to ask for an App Store rating after substantial use), and the network status indicator (to detect when you are offline). These features run on your device and do not transmit personal data to us.

No Accounts, Analytics, or Tracking

Every PL8 does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email address, advertising identifier, or any other personal information. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or third-party tracking domains in this app.

Plate Imagery and Trademarks

Plate images shown in the app belong to their respective state motor vehicle agencies and are displayed for identification, educational, and entertainment purposes under a fair use claim. Logos, mascots, names, and other marks depicted on these plates are the property of their respective owners and are shown solely for identification of the license plates issued by the state agencies.

California specialty plates: California license plate designs, including specialty plates, may incorporate artwork licensed by the California Department of Motor Vehicles from third-party organizations. Such images are displayed solely for identification and educational purposes under a fair use claim (17 U.S.C. § 107). Gorilla Grin makes no claim of affiliation with the California DMV or any organization whose artwork appears on California plates, and no commercial license is claimed or implied.

Children

Every PL8 does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app stores all data locally on the device. If you believe a child has used the app, the data resides only on that device and can be deleted from the in-app Settings.

International Users (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Every PL8 stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit, share, or sell any personal information. We do not process personal data on any server. You can delete all stored data at any time from the in-app Settings, and you may export a backup of your data at any time using the Export Progress feature.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
